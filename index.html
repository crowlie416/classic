<Great Moments in History> is looking to build up our classic raiding team.

With over 10 years raiding experience across all expansions, we’re a “serious raiding guild”-turned-“casual raiding guild” (with the love of min-maxing firmly intact). We offer a relaxed environment of mature players who balance WoW and life while clearing content in a timely and efficient manner.

Raid Schedule

  • Tuesdays and Thursdays
  • 6pm - 9:30pm PST

Raiding Requirements

  • Be knowledgeable about raid encounters, how your class/role can best add value to the team and be open to constructive criticism
  • Be prepared with enchants, consumables and make efforts towards obtaining your pre-raid gear/attunements (the guild will help with this)
  • Be mature and respectful to other raid members.
  • Be reliable: real life happens and we do not expect 100% raid attendance - just be sure to let the raid know when you cannot attend

Guild Recruitment

We're currently recruiting for all roles. We are open to any strong players who share similar goals and values and are happy to assist with getting you ready for raiding.
